ABOUT THE INSTITUTION

The Institution

The Department of Architecture at BRAC University cultivates future designers capable of responding to environmental, social, and urban challenges through innovative architectural thinking.

Our educational framework combines contextual design, sustainability, technology, and research-based studio practices to shape the next generation of creative professionals.

Design Disciplines

The school currently offers certificate and degree programs in:
