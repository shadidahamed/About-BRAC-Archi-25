VISUAL IDENTITY & CONCEPT

The Cyber-Cube Core

The visual identity of the BRAC Architecture Summer '26 platform centers around an isometric hyper-cube. This structure serves as a clean intersection between structural physical spaces and modular digital layouts. It acts as a baseline framework where architectural design seamlessly connects with advanced engineering principles.

Structural Geometry

Represents structural form, precision, and the physical foundations of architectural execution.

Digital Integration

Highlights the interactive framework and modern web technologies used to display creative designs.

Designer’s Note

"Our goal was to design an interactive system that operates beyond a standard website interface. By utilizing deep glassmorphic layers, structured text arrangements, and high-performance crimson light maps, the dashboard reflects the core theme of the studio: clean, functional, and forward-looking."

ABOUT INSTITUTION

BRAC University

BRAC University is an architectural and educational milestone. It stands as a beacon of progressive, sustainable design and visionary leadership.
