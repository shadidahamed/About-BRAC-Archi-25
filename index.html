ABOUT THE BATCH

We are the Architects
of Tomorrow

BRAC University Architecture Batch 25 is a collective of passionate young designers committed to shaping a sustainable, inclusive, and culturally rooted built environment.

Rooted in the ethos of BRAC, we approach architecture not just as a profession, but as a tool for social transformation and environmental responsibility.
