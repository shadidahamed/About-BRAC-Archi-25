BRAC UNIVERSITY ARCHITECTURE

Located in the vibrant metropolis of Dhaka, Bangladesh, the Department of Architecture at BRAC University has stood as a bastion of innovative pedagogy since its inception.

Our curriculum synthesizes rigorous spatial strategy, regional contextualism, and state-of-the-art building science. We guide students toward understanding the unique deltaic complexity of our landscape while engineering global sustainable answers.

Studio Frameworks

Studio VII Thesis Tier Core Focus

The Terminal Synthesis

Studio VII serves as the final launchpad, tasking students with navigating macro urban conditions and proposing architectural interventions for high-density landscapes.

Projects combine deep programmatic complexity with structural realism, dealing directly with regional issues like water logging, resource constraints, and historic landscape patterns.

Studio VI Advanced Tier Tectonic System

Tectonic Systems & Speculation

Studio VI shifts attention directly to the material palette. Students explore structural mechanics and parametric form-generation methodologies.

Focusing deeply on digital manufacturing, material computational loops, and circular loops, items here challenge how material form maps to ecological performance values.
