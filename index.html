ABOUT THE COHORT

Academic Vision & Community Goals

The Department of Architecture at BRAC University trains professionals to face local, regional, and global challenges with creative responsibility.

Our platform indexes, highlights, and preserves the creative achievements, studio projects, and thesis presentations of our students. By bridging computational planning with sustainable construction methods, we seek to develop long-term spatial models that honor context and climate conditions.
